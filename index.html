Pasuram 29 - Andal

From the Thiruppavai – Devotional Verses of Andal

Vratham means – Vow or Resolve.

We are reflecting on Pasuram 29 from the sacred Thiruppavai by Andal.

Pasuram 29 (Devanagari)

IAST/Devanagari script
शिट्रम् शिरुगाले वन्दुन्नै च्चेवित्तु उन् पॊट्रामरै अडिये पोट्रुम् परुळ् केळाय् पॆट्रम् मेय्तुण्णुम् कुलत्तिल् पिरन्दु- नी कुट्रेवल् ऎङ्गळे कलामल पोगादु इट्रै प्परै कॊळ्वान् अन्तु काण कोविन्दा ! ऎट्रैक्कुम् एळेऴ पिरविक्कुम् उन् तन्नो - डुट्रोमे आवोम् उनक्के नाम् आट् चॆय्वोम् मद्रै नम् कामङ्गळ् माद्रेलोर् ऐम् पावाय् ॥ 29 ॥

Pasuram 29 (Tamil)

Commentary

Andal sings pointing at the time when they came to meet Bhagavan. She says — “Hey Bhagavan, we didn't come here during kaalai which is usually the time when the people with tamasa guna wake up to do their daily routine. We did not even come here during shiru kaalai which is usually the time when people with sattva guna wake up to worship You before starting their daily routine. We have come during chittrañ ciṛu kaalai unable to get any sleep at all, because we are unable to bear this separation from You. We have come because Your Divine Feet are far more precious to us than glittering gold. Those Feet are attracting us like how honey in the lotus flowers attracts the bees.

Normally You are the One who rushes to Your devotees whenever they need and You are the One who feels sad if Your devotees come in search of You before You manage to reach them. This time, we have come so far to see You. Having such a vatsalya guṇam, even after knowing that we have come this far — why are You questioning us about our qualifications and intentions? Govinda! Did You think that we wanted to praise You for this one day, and take the drum You gave us and leave? Have You also lost Your intelligence because of going behind those cows? We trust You because You are born in the Yadava kulam like us and it is Your foremost responsibility to protect us and grant us our wishes. Then why are You still hesitating? Now, please listen closely to what we are asking.

We have come here to ask You — that we want to share the same relationship with You as the Nityasooris do. We want the soubhagyam of doing Your personal service always, close to You in Your presence — just like how Adiśeṣa, Garuḍa, Mother Sri Mahalakshmi and all the Nityasooris serve You eternally. Also, we are not asking this boon for this one day. As long as time exists and however many janmas we need to take — in every janma we want to remain in such a close relationship with You. We do not care if we have to be born on earth, heaven or hell. For us every place is Sri Vaikunṭham if You are with us and if we are involved in Your antaranga kainkaryam. We pray to You — please take each one of our little desires and small wishes and convert them all into this one single desire: to be united with You forever.

“எற்றைக்கும் ஏழ் ஏழ் பிறவிக்கும் உன் தன்னோடு உற்றோமே ஆவோம் — for all time and births, may we be united with You.”
