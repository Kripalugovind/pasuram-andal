Commentary

When Bhagavan questions Godha Devi and Ghoshthi about their intentions behind the Vratham and their rights and merits to get the Vratha Phalitam тАУ in yesterday's Pasuram Andal humbly did Kshama Prardhana to Bhagavan Sri Krishna and answered that тАУ all they know is that they have the good fortune to be born in the Gopa kulam as Bhagavan Himself. Today Andal continues to answer the question and talk about what they actually want.

Andal sings pointing at the time when they came to meet Bhagavan. She says тАФ тАЬHey Bhagavan, we didn't come here during kaalai which is usually the time when the people with tamasa guna wake up to do their daily routine. We did not even come here during shiru kaalai which is usually the time when people with sattva guna wake up to worship You before starting their daily routine. We have come during chittra├▒ ciс╣Ыu kaalai unable to get any sleep at all, because we are unable to bear this separation from You. We have come because Your Divine Feet are far more precious to us than glittering gold. Those Feet are attracting us like how honey in the lotus flowers attracts the bees.

Normally You are the One who rushes to Your devotees whenever they need and You are the One who feels sad if Your devotees come in search of You before You manage to reach them. This time, we have come so far to see You. Having such a vatsalya guс╣Зam, even after knowing that we have come this far тАФ why are You questioning us about our qualifications and intentions? Govinda! Did You think that we wanted to praise You for this one day, and take the drum You gave us and leave? Have You also lost Your intelligence because of going behind those cows? We trust You because You are born in the Yadava kulam like us and it is Your foremost responsibility to protect us and grant us our wishes. Then why are You still hesitating? Now, please listen closely to what we are asking.

We have come here to ask You тАФ that we want to share the same relationship with You as the Nityasooris do. We want the soubhagyam of doing Your personal service always, close to You in Your presence тАФ just like how Adi┼Ыeс╣гa, Garuс╕Нa, Mother Sri Mahalakshmi and all the Nityasooris serve You eternally. We do not care if we have to be born on earth, heaven or hell. For us every place is Sri Vaikunс╣нham if You are with us and if we are involved in Your antaranga kainkaryam. We pray to You тАФ please take each one of our little desires and small wishes and convert them all into this one single desire: to be united with You forever.
